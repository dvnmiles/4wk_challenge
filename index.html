Timed Quiz on JavaScript Fundamentals

Quiz Rules

1. You will be given 100 seconds to complete all the questions in the quiz.
2. When you click on your answer, your response will be recorded if the console storage.
3. You will not be able to change your response, so choose wisely. One could say its kind of like one of those games in Squid Games.
4. You will see some feedback on the screen that will tell you if you got the right or wrong answer.
5. IMPORTANT: If you choose an incorrect response 20 seconds will be deducted from the time remaining.
6. To keep things fair and to mitigate any festering cases of test taker anxiety, the time clock will stop counting down after you select each answer and it will resume counting down once you click the next question button.
7. When you either answer all the questions or run out of time the quiz will end.
8. Any questions left unanswered will be marked as incorrect.
9. At the end of the quiz you will be presented with your score.
10. When your score is presented, you will have the option to save your score and initials to the console storage.
